About Me:

I'm the wonderful hybrid of designer and developer. I understand the value of both sides and strive to make sure that my websites and applications follow the best practices throughout the whole process. Frontend development is always a bit of a fun passion for me, but I love the challenges that are often presented with backend development and computational programming.
